Background

Seven-Up bottling company Ltd is one of the largest manufacturing companies in Nigeria, producing and distributing some of the favourite most-loved soft drink brands in the country like; Seven-Up, Pepsi, Mirinda, Mountain Dew, H2oH!, Teem and Aquafina bottled water.

Established in 1959 with its first product 7up, rolled out on October 1, 1960. Making the organization over 59years strong in business building and creating a brand loved by the Nigerian community, thereby becoming the leading CSD manufacturer in Nigeria.

SBC boasts of nine bottling plants with state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities strategically located across various regions in the country.
